Tips & Tricks

Credits: the Freak, AsheyXD, leatyDS, xLiam1, Seki, Stewart

Pre-Scoping + Pre-firing for Advantage

For all weapons except shotguns and especially the AWP try pre-scoping common spawn points. If no one appears, quickly release your aim to reset your focus.

Perfecting AWP Crosshair Placement

Keep your crosshair at head level relative to the ground. This allows you to focus mainly on horizontal aim rather than vertical adjustments.

Check Your Ping

Type /ping in the Deadshot.io chat during gameplay to view your current ping and assess your connection.

Air Movement Combat Trick

If you spot an enemy close by, jump over them and rotate 180° mid-air. This forces them to adjust their aim while you can shoot faster upon landing.

Map Awareness and Spawn Control

Enemies won't spawn if you're within roughly 10 - 20 meters of a spawn point. Control one side of the map to limit their spawns and expect new enemies within five seconds after each kill.

Slide Shooting

When slide shooting, aim slightly above the head before sliding. Use higher sensitivity for wider strafes and aim just before landing for precision.

Fake Combat

Empty AR and SMG magazines into a wall to fake combat.

Weapon Matchups

Use shotguns versus snipers, SMGs/ARs versus shotguns, and snipers against everyone.

Movement Basics

Against shotguns or SMGs, perform 180° slide jumps. Against snipers or ARs, strafe side to side mid-air.

Headshot Alignment

In Deadshot.io, hitboxes are slightly taller than models. Aim just above the head for more headshots.
